      • Buffalo Express
        $ Burgers, American (Traditional)
        11090 Santa Monica Blvd
        Los Angeles, CA 90025
        3/17/2015

        This location is co-branded with Fatburger. I'm not a fan of Fatburger but decided to give it a try. They offer online ordering through their Buffalo Express website and also Grubhub. I noticed that Grubhub included a combo deal for chicken tenders and curly fries so I ordered with them.
        The location has quirky but appealing style. It felt new and looked very clean. The staff were friendly and there's a padded bench in front of the checkout where you can sit while you wait for your order.
        The meal was hot and the chicken tasted good, not like it was mass produced. The sauces were light which I find appealing. The curly fries were also good although a few were undercooked.
        Their soda vending machine is one of the touch screen models with multiple brands available. I finally go to try Dr. Pepper Cherry!
        I can easily see myself going back to try some of their other items.
